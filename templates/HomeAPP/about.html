About LapExpress

Your trusted destination for modern laptops and reliable technology

LapExpress is a laptop-focused e-commerce platform built to simplify online laptop shopping. The platform allows users to explore products, view detailed specifications, manage carts, and place orders securely.

The application is developed using Django following MVT architecture, with a clean and responsive user interface. Staff-restricted functionality enables authorized users to manage products and content efficiently.
